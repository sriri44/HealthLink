Essential Health Guidelines

Children (0-12 years)

  • Balanced nutrition with essential vitamins and minerals
  • Regular immunizations and health check-ups
  • 60 minutes of active play daily
  • 9-12 hours of quality sleep
  • Limited screen time and outdoor activities

Teens (13-19 years)

  • Nutrient-rich diet for growth and development
  • Regular physical activity and sports
  • Mental health awareness and support
  • 8-10 hours of consistent sleep
  • Balanced academic and social life

Adults (20-59 years)

  • Balanced diet with portion control
  • Regular exercise and fitness routine
  • Stress management and mental wellness
  • Regular health screenings
  • Work-life balance maintenance

Seniors (60+ years)

  • Nutrient-dense, easy-to-digest meals
  • Gentle exercise and mobility activities
  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Social engagement and mental stimulation
  • Medication management and care
